About Me

Mary Liang Buckley

Kia ora! I am a Registered Nurse and self-taught software developer from Seattle, Washington but most recently lived in New Zealand for the past five years. My professional background for the past decade has been in healthcare, specifically as a nurse in pediatric hospitals. However, after experiencing burnout and the acute challenges brought on by the COVID-19 pandemic within the healthcare industry, I decided it was time to explore different career options that support a healthier work-life balance and offer greater work flexibility. So I took a leap of faith and pivoted to software development and the IT field. My goal is to find a remote job so I can continue travelling while working remotely. I hope my story, though unconventional, will inspire other motivated women and former healthcare professionals to enter the exciting world of tech and find roles where they can reach their full potential and thrive.

I discovered SheCodes in 2022. It was a great opportunity to take my first steps into coding. Since then, I've completed all of the workshops and have become proficient with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.js. In January 2023, I completed a software engineering internship with Concert Health, a leading behavioral health medical group in the US. I acquired hands-on experience working on real world problems and designed solutions for user-facing applications within a team of engineers in an async environment. Most recently, I entered the Salesforce ecosystem as a Certified Salesforce Administrator and aspiring Salesforce Business Analyst. I've been on an incredible journey with Salesforce and look forward to working in these exciting roles. I love being able to wear many different hats and fully believe in maintaining a growth mindset throughout my learning journey and continuous professional development.

According to Adobe's Creative Type test, I am The Visionary: passion-driven with a keen interest in the world around me and a desire to contribute to society. I live preferring to see things not as they are but as they could be and creating a community around shared values and ideas. I'm a passionate advocate for healthcare equity, environmental conservation, and community building. From my extensive nursing career, I possess a combination of strong technical and soft skillsets that include: critical thinking, data analysis, multi-tasking in a fast-paced work environment, cross-functional collobaration, empathy, and communication. My hope is to leverage my many trasnferable skills along with my new set of IT skills to deliver quality products and create positive user experiences. In my free time I love surfing, being out in nature, reading books, and travelling.
