About Me

Mary Liang Buckley

I am a self-taught web developer from Seattle, Washington but lived in New Zealand for the past four years. Currently, I'm a digital nomad travelling with my husband. My professional background has primarily been in inpatient healthcare, specifically as a registered nurse in pediatric hospitals for the past 10 years. However, after experiencing burnout and the acute challenges created by the pandemic, I decided it was time to explore other career options that support a better work-life balance. So I took a leap of faith and pivoted to software development. My goal is to find a remote job so I can continue to work and travel. I hope my story, though unconventional, will inspire other motivated women to enter the exciting world of tech and find roles where they can thrive.

I discovered SheCodes in the spring of 2022. It came at the perfect time and it was a great opportunity to take my first steps into coding. Since then, I've completed the full bootcamp and become proficient with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React JS. It's been a demanding but rewarding learning journey thus far and I'm eager to continue growing my development toolkit. I'm a passionate advocate for healthcare equity, social justice issues, and community building. My goal is to build products and applications that directly help and empower people and their surrounding communities. I am highly adaptable and love looking at the bigger picture to solve problems. From my nursing experience, I possess strong critical thinking, analytical, and communication skills. I'm currently an engineering intern at Concert Health, a leading behavioral health medical group in the US.
