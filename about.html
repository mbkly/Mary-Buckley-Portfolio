About Me

Mary Liang Buckley

Kia ora! I am a self-taught web developer from Seattle, Washington but lived in New Zealand for the past five years. Currently, I'm travelling with my husband around the world. My professional background for the past decade has been in healthcare, specifically as a registered nurse in pediatric hospitals. However, after experiencing burnout and the acute challenges brought on by the COVID-19 pandemic within the healthcare industry, I decided it was time to explore different career options that support a healthier work-life balance and offer greater work flexibility. So I took a leap of faith and pivoted to software development and the IT field. My goal is to find a remote job so I can continue travelling while working remotely as a digital nomad. I hope my story, though unconventional, will inspire other motivated women to enter the exciting world of tech and find roles where they can reach their full potential and thrive.

I discovered SheCodes in 2022. It came at the perfect time and it was a great opportunity to take my first steps into coding. Since then, I've completed all the workshops and become proficient with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React JS. It's been a demanding but rewarding learning journey and I'm eager to continue growing my development toolkit. From my extensive nursing experience, I possess a combination of strong technical and soft skillsets that include: critical thinking, data analysis, multi-tasking in a fast-paced work environment, cross-functional collobaration, empathy, advocacy, and communication. In 2023, I completed a software engineering internship with Concert Health, a leading behavioral health medical group in the US. I acquired hands-on experience working on real world problems and creating solutions to user-facing applications within a team of engineers and product managers in a remote, async environment. I'm currently studying the Salesforce ecosystem and working toward getting my Salesforce Administrator certification to further my IT horizons. Finally, product management has also recently piqued my interest and I'm currently enrolled in a Udemy course to learn more about this field.

According to Adobe's Creative Type test, I am The Visionary: passion-driven with a keen interest in the world around me and a desire to contribute to society. I live preferring to see things not as they are but as they could be and creating a community around shared values and ideas. I'm a passionate advocate for healthcare equity, social justice issues, and community building. My hope is to leverage my deep healthcare background plus my new set of web development/IT skills to build products and user experiences that directly help and empower people and their surrounding communities. In my free time I love surfing, being out in nature, reading books, spending time with family and friends, and travelling.
